# Adolf Hitler
Adolf Hitler (**1889â€“1945**) was [[Chancellor]] of Germany from **1933** to **1945** and dictator of Nazi Germany from **1934** to **1945**. After Hitler was appointed [[Chancellor]], he immediately transformed the Weimar Republic into the [[Third Reich]]. He wanted to establish a "New Order" and create more "living space" for what he believed was the superior Aryan race. Hitler aggressively invaded neighboring nations. which led to World War II. As Germany faced defeat in 1945, Hitler committed suicide to avoid capture by the Soviets.
